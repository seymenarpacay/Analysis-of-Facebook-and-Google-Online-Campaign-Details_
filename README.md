# Analysis-of-Facebook-and-Google-Online-Campaign-Details_
In this project, I conducted a comprehensive performance analysis using multi-channel (Google and Facebook) digital marketing data. I processed the spending, reach, and revenue metrics in the dataset through daily performance rankings focused on ROMI (Return on Investment), weekly campaign-based value records, and monthly growth trends. Specifically, by measuring the continuity of ad sets through "continuous impression" analysis, I established a structure that combines data analytics capabilities with marketing optimization and decision support processes.
